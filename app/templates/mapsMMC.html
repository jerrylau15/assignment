MOViE MOViE Cityplaza

The first cinema concept in Hong Kong that features culture and lifestyle. Get the ultimate theatrical experience and live movies with us!

Broadway Circuit is proud to announce the opening of our ground-breaking MOViE MOViE Cityplaza location in 2018. The cinema is the first of its kind to feature “culture” and “lifestyle” as major design elements. Our new cinema boasts 980 seats in 7 auditoriums, and aims to create a cozy, all-inclusive, dine in cinema for moviegoers. Inheriting the spirit of Broadway Cinematheque, there will always be something for everyone as MOViE MOViE Cityplaza will present an extensive programme of masterpieces ranging from award-winning blockbusters to renowned film-festival selections. The interior of the cinema has aesthetically-pleasing interior design, with exhibition spaces for art, history and multimedia that promote audience interaction.

At MOViE MOViE Cityplaza, 2 auditoriums are Atmos cinemas, or Atmos House, that boasts advanced sound systems. At the new VIP House, moviegoers can even share every important movie moment with 16 friends and family through MM Moments.

Premium vibrating seats are available across all our auditoriums (except the VIP House), which surely help provide a stimulating movie experience for the audience. In terms of visual technology, the cutting-edge RealD Ultimate Screen technology enhances picture brightness efficiency by 80%, making screens appear bigger. It has the industry’s highest stereo contrast ratio and Nano-perforations eliminate ghosting and speckles. This coupled with the advanced 4K Laser Projection, Broadway Circuit promises a theatrical experience like no other at MOViE MOViE Cityplaza.

At the pinnacle of our offerings, the MOViEMAXX auditorium has 270 seats and deploys 40LHC high contrast 4K laser projector, delivering a native contrast ratio of 6,000:1 for some truly refined and spectacular imagery. Also, the revolutionary AuroMax 3D sound system also delivers a concentrated and layered, yet delightfully natural sound spread.
